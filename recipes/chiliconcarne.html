Chili con Carne

Back to recipe page

Description

This recipe can easily be multiplied and freezes well
It is ready in about 1hr5mins and is easy to prepare.

Ingredients (10 servings)

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 large chopped onions
  • 1 chopped chili pepper
  • 5 chopped cloves garlic
  • 2 pound lean ground beef
  • 3 (14.5 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes
  • 0.5 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1.5 tablespoons ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 (15.25 ounce) cans red kidney beans, rinsed and drained

Steps

  1. In a medium sized stock pot, heat the oil over medium heat
  2. Saute onion, chili, pepper and garlic until soft
  3. Add ground beef
  4. Cook and stir until meat is browned
  5. Pour in tomatoes with water or broth
  6. Add salt, pepper, cumin, chili powder, paprika, oregano, cinnamon sticks
  7. Cover and simmer for 45 minutes
  8. Stir in kidney beans and cook another 15 minutes
  9. Remove cinnamon sticks before serving

Nutrition facts

Per Serving: 397 calories; 23.9g protein; 32.5g carbohydrates; 19.3g fat
