Breakfast Bowl

Back to recipe page

Description

This skyr bowl is perfect for breakfast and filled with lots of protein while being low calorie. Perfect in a diet!
It is ready in about 5 minutes and easy to prepare.

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 450g of skyr
  • 50g of blueberries
  • 550g of raspberries
  • 1 teaspoon of honey
  • 25g of protein powder
  • 50g of oatmeal
  • 1 apple
  • 10g of walnuts

Steps

  1. Add skyr to bowl
  2. Add oatmeal and protein powder and mix well
  3. Add honey
  4. Add fruits
  5. Top with walnuts

Nutrition facts

Per Serving: 712 calories; 56.5g protein; 40.2g carbohydrates; 15.3g fat
