Red Thai Chicken Curry

Back to recipe page

Description

This Thai Curry is filled with tons of flavor!
It is ready in about 20 minutes and easy to prepare.

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast - cut into thin slices
  • 1 tablespoon of Thai red curry paste
  • 1 fresh red chili peper, seeded and minced
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced
  • 1 onion, cut into small cubes
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1 (14 ounce) can light coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 lime

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onion and stir for about 2 minutes
  3. Mix in chicken and cook for another 3 minutes
  4. Now add curry paste, bell pepper and carrot. Sir for a few minutes
  5. Dissolve the cornstarch in the coconut milk
  6. Pour into the skillet
  7. Bring to a boil and simmer over medium heat for 2 minutes
  8. Right before serving, stir in the cilantro
  9. Finish with a squeeze of lime

Nutrition facts

Per Serving: 271 calories; 25.4g protein; 11.2g carbohydrates; 15.8g fat
